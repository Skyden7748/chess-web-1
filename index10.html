Job Description

Chessensei is a rapidly growing online & offline chess training platform that is looking for experienced and passionate chess trainers to join our team. We are seeking individuals who are dedicated to helping our students improve their chess skills and achieve their goals.

Responsibilities

Provide Online chess training to students of all levels and ages, Create and deliver customized lesson plans to meet the individual needs of students, Provide feedback and guidance on student progress and areas for improvement, Communicate regularly with students and parents to ensure their satisfaction with the training, Stay current with the latest chess training techniques and strategies.

Qualifications

Minimum of 6months of experience as a chess trainer, Strong chess knowledge and ability to play at a high level ,Excellent communication and teaching skills, Ability to work well in a offline remote, online environment, Passion for chess and desire to help others improve their skills, FIDE rating is a plus.
